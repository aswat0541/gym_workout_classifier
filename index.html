🏋️ Gym Exercise Experience Level Prediction

Machine Learning + Deep Learning for personalised workout recommendations

📌 Problem Statement

Predict a user's experience level (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced) based on gym session data such as age, BMI, heart rate, calories burned, workout type, and frequency. This helps fitness apps personalise training plans and prevent injuries.

📊 Dataset

Source: Synthetic gym tracking data (973 records, 15 features).

Features: Age, Gender, Weight, Height, Max_BPM, Avg_BPM, Resting_BPM, Session Duration, Calories Burned, Workout Type, Fat Percentage, Water Intake, Workout Frequency, BMI (engineered BMI_Class and HR_Burn_Ratio).

Target: Experience_Level (1 = Beginner, 2 = Intermediate, 3 = Advanced).

🤖 Models Implemented

  • Machine Learning: Random Forest, Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, SVM, KNN (with hyperparameter tuning).
  • Deep Learning: 3-layer Neural Network (128-64-32 neurons) with Dropout, BatchNormalisation, EarlyStopping, and ReduceLROnPlateau.
